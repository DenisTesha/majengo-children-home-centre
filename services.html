What We Offer

At Majengo Children Home Centre, we provide a range of services to ensure the well-being and development of the children in our care:

  • Shelter and Housing: A safe and comfortable living environment for every child.
  • Educational Support: Access to quality education and educational materials.
  • Healthcare Services: Regular medical check-ups and access to healthcare facilities.
  • Emotional Support: Counseling and emotional care to help children heal from past traumas.

Our Approach

We believe in a holistic approach to child welfare. By addressing the physical, emotional, and educational needs of each child, we aim to provide them with the tools and opportunities they need to thrive and become successful adults.
