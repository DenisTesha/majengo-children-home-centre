Make a Difference

Your generous donation can help support the children at Majengo Children Home Centre. Your contribution goes a long way in providing them with a brighter future.

Ways to Donate

You can donate in the following ways:

  • Online Donation: Use our secure online donation form to make a one-time or recurring contribution.
  • Bank Transfer: You can transfer funds directly to our bank account. Contact us for details.
  • In-Person Donation: Visit us at our center to make a donation in person.
