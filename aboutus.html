Our Mission

Majengo Children Center is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned and vulnerable children. Our mission is to transform their lives through education, healthcare, and emotional support, ensuring they have the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Story

Founded in [2008], Majengo Children Center has been a haven for countless children who have faced unimaginable challenges in their young lives. Through the dedication of our staff and the support of our donors, we have been able to create a loving and supportive community for these children.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals who are passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of children. We have experienced caregivers, educators, and healthcare providers who work together to ensure the well-being of every child under our care.
